Stop going to SeaWorld!

All you have to do to help is stop going to SeaWorld! Spread the word. Make sure everyone knows how SeaWorld treats their animals. There are many petitions to stop SeaWorld, but with no real results. As long as people still go to the parks, they will continue to keep them open. We need to inform people so that they will stop giving SeaWorld money to keep orcas and other animals in their parks. If everyone stops going, SeaWorld would have no option but to call it quits and shut down the parks.
